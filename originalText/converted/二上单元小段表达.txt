Please go to bed early.
It’s nine o’clock. It’s time to go to bed. But the little rabbit wants to play. It’s seven o’clock in the morning. It’s time to get up. She is sleepy. It’s eleven o’clock in the morning. It’s time to play. Everyone is happy. But she doesn’t want to play. She wants to sleep. Miss Bear says please go to bed early in the evening.
词汇：But -- but但是 want – wants 想要
I go to school on an elephant today
Everyone goes to school in the morning. Bear goes to school by bike. It’s fast. Tortoise walks to school. It’s 8:10. So Tortoise will be late. Elephant can help him. Tortoise says he goes to school on an elephant today.
词汇：go - goes 去  him 他  say – says 说
A super player
Yangyang and Zhuangzhuang like football. They play together. Yangyang runs fast. Zhuangzhuang runs slowly. But Zhuangzhuang can catch the ball. Because he can jump high. He is a good player.

A nice week
Lele gets a little dog on Monday. He is happy. On Tuesday, he draws a house for his dog. On Wednesday and Thursday, he and his dad build a house for the dog. On Friday, he paints the house. Here comes the weekend. His dog can play in his new house. What a nice week!
词汇：His – his 他的    get – get 得到   draw -draws 画画  
paint – paints  涂色  build - builds 建造
Sunday is a big day
